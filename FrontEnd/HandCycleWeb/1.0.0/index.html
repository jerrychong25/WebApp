Welcome to MySIGHT4Rehab

The voluntary group is working on "Low-Cost Upper Extremities Rehabilitation System with Interactive Feedback for Children with Movement Disorders".

The project is backed by the IEEE-EMBS Malaysia Chapter, IEEE Engineering Projects in Community Service (IEEE-EPICS), and most importantly volunteers from both IEEE communities alongside with volunteers with same interest.
